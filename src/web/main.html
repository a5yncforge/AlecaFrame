Welcome to AlecaFrame!
Welcome to AlecaFrame
AlecaFrame has a lot of features, lets explore them together!
The first tab is the Foundry, where you can check your overall progress in the game and what you need to complete everything else.
If you click on any Warframe or Weapon in the foundry, you can see more details and where each component can be obtained.
The next tab is the Inventory, where you can see all the tradeable items in your inventory and their average price.
If you connect your WFMarket account, you can create listings with 1 click!
This tab is called the "Relic planner", where you can easily check which relic is the most profitable or recommended for you.
If you click on a relic, you can also see detailed information such as drop locations and customized reward probabilities.
In the next tab, you can check your rivens, see how perfect they are and create listings with 1 click!
The final tab shows your current WFMarket listings, allows you to quickly edit or remove then and even to automatically remove/fix the ones where you don't own the items anymore.
Once you open a relic in-game or need to choose a relic, you will see an overlay with useful information to help you make the best choice.
AlecaFrame can also automatically mark WFMarket listings as sold whenever you trade an item.
You CAN DISABLE IT in the settings menu if you prefer to do it manually.
And we are done here, just make sure that the connection to the game is working to get the most out of the app!
